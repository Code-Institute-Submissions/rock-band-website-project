Guns N' Roses is an American hard rock band from Los Angeles, California, formed in 1985. The lineup consisted of vocalist Axl Rose, lead guitarist Slash, rhythm guitarist Izzy Stradlin, bassist Duff McKagan, and drummer Steven Adler. Guns N' Roses has released six studio albums, accumulating sales of more than 100 million records worldwide, making them the 41st best-selling artist of all time.
